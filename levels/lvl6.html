Chapter 7: I Seem To Have Forgotten Myself(completed)

“WHO DARES DISTURB ME !?”, thundered a voice from the near the hearth. Frightened, you step forward, “Are you the wizard that hails from Karoun, all powerful and knowing?”

“I DO NOT KNOW WHO I AM! DO YOU?”, comes the response.

A cat on the mantle suddenly speaks:

“A spell he cast, to bind and break

A charm that was to memory take,

Backfire it did on the wizard age old,

And now he can’t tell roof from floor,

Match the tiles which form a pair of two,

Repair the wizard is all I ask of you.”
