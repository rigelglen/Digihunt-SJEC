Chapter 1: Meet Her Highness, Andromeda.

In a distant kingdom ruled by benevolent royalty, a free-spirited queen resided in the palace made of crystal and gold. Queen Andromeda, for she was named after the stars, was raised as a warrior, but had a soul as gentle as that of a fawn. The kingdom was prosperous and had nothing to fear, except a beast of a terrible temperament. It was rumoured that this monster could flatten an entire mountain if it was enraged. Many brave knights had set out to vanquish it, but none had lived to tell the tale.

Queen Andromeda feared for her people, and she has approached you and your team of mercenaries to find and kill this dragon.
