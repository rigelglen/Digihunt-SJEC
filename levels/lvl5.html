The forest has been conquered, and you’ve escaped its claws. Well done, the Gods seem to have you in their favour. Now, before you stands the Grand Stardew Tower, in all its glory. You the seek the wizard Hanoi from the magical realm of Karoun, who lives at the top of this formidable monilith. To get to the top, you’ll need a magical ladder, which you can obtain by conquering the problem presented to you.

Instructions:

  2. All the disks should be on a single peg.
